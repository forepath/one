Turn infrastructure complexity into IT you can rely on

Reliable infrastructure comes from open technologies, clear processes, and partners who treat operations and security as one discipline. We run the systems your business depends on, from the office floor to the data centre, so your teams stay productive without constant firefighting.

Network

We design, operate, and maintain your LAN, WAN, Wi-Fi, and firewall estate with documented changes and clear capacity planning.

  • Firewall rule management with scheduled change windows
  • Wi-Fi coverage surveys and ongoing performance tuning
  • WAN link monitoring with regular failover testing

Servers

Physical and virtual servers stay patched, monitored, and rightsized throughout their full lifecycle.

  • Operating system patching and vulnerability remediation
  • Performance baselining with regular capacity reviews
  • Hardware warranty tracking and lifecycle reporting

Workstations

Laptops and desktops are provisioned consistently and supported through refresh cycles at every location.

  • Standardized imaging with approved software baselines
  • Remote and on-site support with defined response targets
  • Asset tracking including lease return and disposal processes

Access & entry control

Digital and physical access policies are enforced so the right people reach the right resources at the right time.

  • Identity reviews and role based access administration
  • Badge and door access provisioning with audit trails
  • Privileged account monitoring and periodic recertification

Telephony & communication

Voice, Teams, and unified communication platforms stay integrated, correctly licensed, and ready for everyday use.

  • Number porting, trunk configuration, and dial plan maintenance
  • Microsoft Teams policies plus meeting room support
  • Call quality monitoring with a clear escalation path

Backup & archiving

Backup jobs, retention policies, and restore procedures are tested against your compliance and recovery targets.

  • Backup success monitoring with proactive alerting
  • Scheduled restore drills with documented outcomes
  • Retention and archival policy enforcement across systems

Security & endpoint protection

Endpoints and users are protected through layered controls and a documented response process.

  • Endpoint detection and antivirus policy management
  • Security patch compliance with regular reporting
  • Phishing triage and incident response runbooks

Digital workplace

Collaboration tools and productivity platforms are kept current so employees can work without daily IT friction.

  • Microsoft 365 tenant administration and policy updates
  • License optimisation with periodic entitlement reviews
  • Shared workspace and file access governance

Virtualization

VMware, Hyper-V, Proxmox, and container hosts run with resilience planning and documented recovery steps.

  • Cluster health monitoring with coordinated patching
  • Snapshot, migration, and failover procedures
  • Capacity planning for seasonal and project driven peaks

Monitoring & management

Dashboards, alerts, and ticket workflows are tuned so issues surface early and teams resolve them faster.

  • Alert threshold tuning to reduce noise and fatigue
  • SLA aligned ticket routing with clear escalation paths
  • Monthly operational health reports for stakeholders
